Team Philosophy

Unity of Knowledge and Action

Action always takes precedence over perfection. Rather than a long wait, we prefer to bring ideas to life quickly and validate their functional value in real-world scenarios.

Simplicity and Humility

We reject over-design and complex internal conflicts. Maintaining simplicity, low cost, and high efficiency are our core principles for combating development chaos.

Going with the Flow

User needs and immediate feedback determine the direction of a project. We dynamically adjust the development priority of features based on actual usage scenarios.

Continuous Growth

Every release is not the end, but the beginning of evolution. We optimize our products through continuous iteration and maintain their vitality amidst change.

About Us
Dubhe Nexus Innovation and Research Studio

Dubhe Nexus is an independent development team focused on the field of flight simulation. We started as a large community platform and have now transformed into an agile development model centered on "lightweight tools + data services".

Our core businesses include:Aviation Data API(Provides professional and easy-to-use public data interfaces)、 Community Operation System and many others Utility tools. We are not simply piling up products; we are searching for the optimal path to solve industry pain points.

The team believes in the values ​​of "simplicity, efficiency, and practicality." Our strategy is very clear: rapid build, low-cost maintenance, feedback-driven, and avoiding introducing unnecessary complexity in the early stages of a project.

Agile Development Space

Dubhe Nexus does not pursue commercial scale; we value the flexibility and practical application of our technology. Our project implementation approach leans towards a lightweight model, aiming to meet the real needs of flight simulation enthusiasts and developers through rapid iteration.

Service Guarantee

Although the project is in its growth stage, some interfaces and functions may be optimized and adjusted according to the technical architecture, but we will do our best to ensure the stability of core services. We welcome suggestions and feedback from our users.
